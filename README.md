# TollRoad Music

![TollRoad Music Logo](https://www.tollroadmusic.xyz/logo.png)

TollRoad Music is platform for independent artists and ip holders to charge pay as you go direct to consumer utalizing x402 and decentralized storage on Filecoin.

## Get Started

Go to www.tollroadmusic.xyz and sign up for an account as either

### Artist
    1. sign up using coinbase embeded wallet flow
    2. upload music
    3. setup your contracts
    4. deploy and earn
### Listener
    1. Sign Up using Coinbase embeded wallet flow
    2. fund your account with usd
    3. find and stream music
