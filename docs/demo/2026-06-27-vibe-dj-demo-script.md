# TollRoad — Demo Video Shooting Script

**Date:** 2026-06-27
**Runtime target:** ~2:45 (hard ceiling 3:00)
**Format:** Consumer-first 3-act — Hook → Proof → Vision
**Cameras:** Sony A7III (founder-to-camera, shallow-DOF interview), DJI / 360 (hands, fan, device B-roll), screen capture (real product), AI visual / motion-gfx (concept + scale)

---

## Throughline (the one thing every frame serves)

> *A new music platform where fans earn real, tracked rewards for listening to the artists they love — streaks, leaderboards, a real bond — giving artists a new way to monetize and engage their fanbase.*

This is a **consumer product** about fans and artists. Everything serves that relationship. The AI-agent capability is real and impressive, but in this film it's **one line near the end** — a side-mention, not the spine.

## Visual treatment legend

| Tag | Meaning | Source |
|---|---|---|
| 🎤 | Founder to camera | Sony A7III, 50mm-ish, shallow DOF, soft key, dark backdrop |
| 🤚 | Fan / hands / device B-roll | DJI / 360 — a fan with headphones, phone in hand, the app, everyday context |
| 🖥️ | Real screen capture | Actual product: player, streak counter, leaderboard, rewards, live meter |
| ✨ | AI visual / motion-gfx | Generated B-roll + animated scale/concept visuals |

**Golden rule for honesty:** anything tagged 🖥️ must be a *real, unedited* recording (numbers may be pre-seeded for a clean run, but the feature is genuine). Anything conceptual or future-tense is ✨ and should read as illustration, never as a fake screenshot. See "What's real vs. illustrated" at the bottom.

## Spoken-line budget

Three founder lines, each deliverable in one breath (≤10s). Total VO across the film ≈ 300 words at an unhurried ~140 wpm. Let the music and the real product breathe; resist wall-to-wall narration.

# ACT 1 — HOOK · "Your listening finally counts" (0:00–0:45)

### Beat 1.1 — Cold open: press play (0:00–0:10)
- **Visual:** 🤚 A fan, headphones on, thumb hits play on a phone — an artist they clearly love (poster, merch, a face lighting up). Intimate, warm, real.
- **On-screen text:** *(none yet)*
- **Audio:** the artist's track kicks in on the press. Music carries the open.
- **VO:** *(none — let the moment land)*

### Beat 1.2 — This time it's tracked (0:10–0:30)
- **Visual:** 🖥️ Real product. As the track plays, a **streak counter ticks up** and a small **reward accrues** on screen — listening is being *counted* for the first time. Cut between the real UI and 🤚 the fan noticing.
- **On-screen text:** lower-third: `listening streak · day 12` / `+rewards earned`
- **VO (narrator):** *"You've listened to this artist for years. For the first time, it counts for something."*

### Beat 1.3 — The bond, stated (0:30–0:45)
- **Visual:** 🎤 Founder to camera, intercut with 🖥️ the streak/reward UI.
- **VO (🎤 founder, Line 1 — to camera):** *"We're building a place where being a fan is finally rewarded — your listening, tracked and turned into something real."*
- **Audio:** music swells; first **TollRoad** wordmark can appear small in the corner.

# ACT 2 — PROOF · "Real rewards, real rankings, real support" (0:45–2:00)

### Beat 2.1 — Streaks that grow (0:45–1:05)
- **Visual:** 🖥️ Real product, hero shot: the **listening streak** building day over day, rewards stacking. Filmed live — it moves because real listening drove it.
- **On-screen text:** `streak · day 12 → day 13` / `rewards: unlocked`
- **VO (narrator):** *"Every minute you listen builds your streak — and earns rewards you actually keep."*

### Beat 2.2 — Climb the leaderboard (1:05–1:30)
- **Visual:** 🖥️ Real **leaderboard** of an artist's top fans — your handle climbing the ranks. Cut to 🤚 the fan reacting to moving up.
- **On-screen text:** `Top fans · @you ↑ #7` / `<artist> superfans`
- **VO (narrator):** *"Climb the leaderboard for the artists you love. Real fandom, finally ranked — and seen."*

### Beat 2.3 — The artist side (1:30–1:55)
- **Visual:** 🎤 Founder to camera (credibility anchor), intercut with 🤚 a creator/studio detail and ✨ a clean motion-gfx of listening-seconds flowing to an artist.
- **VO (🎤 founder, Line 2 — to camera):** *"And every second you listen is tracked — so artists get paid for real engagement, and finally see exactly who their superfans are."*
- **Note:** the per-second payout ledger is real backend, but here it's *illustrated* (✨), not screen-captured — keep it as concept so we never imply a fake screenshot.

### Beat 2.4 — Discover by vibe (1:55–2:00)
- **Visual:** 🖥️ Quick beat — type a mood (`"late-night drive"`) → genuinely matching tracks return. Listening, and the rewards that follow, start from a feeling.
- **On-screen text:** `find the vibe · keep the streak`
- **VO:** *(none — let the cut breathe)*

# ACT 3 — VISION · "A new model for fans and artists" (2:00–2:45)

### Beat 3.1 — At scale (2:00–2:20)
- **Visual:** ✨ Confident montage: many fans, many artists, streaks and leaderboards everywhere — the feel of a platform at Spotify scale, but where listening is rewarded and artists are directly supported.
- **On-screen text:** `millions of fans · paid artists · real rewards`
- **VO (narrator):** *"A new kind of music platform — where listening rewards fans, and engagement pays the artists who earned it."*
- **AI-visual prompt seed:** *"Sweeping cinematic montage of diverse music fans listening on phones and headphones in everyday places, intercut with artists performing and seeing fan stats light up, warm premium color grade, sense of massive scale, uplifting."*

### Beat 3.2 — The agent side-mention (2:20–2:30)
- **Visual:** ✨ One quick, distinct graphic — a small "for developers & AI agents" card — clearly a footnote, not the headline.
- **On-screen text:** `metered to the second → API & MCP ready`
- **VO (narrator, brisk):** *"It's metered to the second — so it's ready for apps and AI agents to license this music too."*
- **Note:** ONE line, then immediately back to fans/artists. Do not linger.

### Beat 3.3 — The close (2:30–2:45)
- **Visual:** 🎤 Founder to camera, locked-off, final line → end card.
- **VO (🎤 founder, Line 3 — to camera):** *"We're redefining how fans and artists connect — listening that rewards you, and a fanbase artists can finally build on."*
- **End card (✨):** **TollRoad** wordmark · tagline: *"Your listening, finally rewarded."* · one CTA line (URL / "Join the waitlist").
- **Audio:** music resolves cleanly on the wordmark. Hard out — no fade-to-mush.

## What's real vs. illustrated (keep this honest with judges)

**Real, filmed live (🖥️):** listening **streaks**, the **leaderboard**, **tracked rewards**, the player streaming real audio, and vibe/mood discovery. Confirmed working today.

**Real backend, but illustrated (✨) rather than screen-captured:** the per-second listening → artist payout/royalty ledger. It runs, but we depict it as a clean motion-graphic so we never imply a fake UI.

**Vision (✨), clearly stylized:** the scale montage (platform at Spotify scale) and the "API & MCP ready" footnote — the agent capability is genuinely built, but in this film it's intentionally a one-line side-mention.

If a judge asks "is that real?" the answer for every 🖥️ beat is **yes, that's the running product** — which is why we lead with the fan experience, not a pitch.
